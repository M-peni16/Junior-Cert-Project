The Damages of Climate change

So what are the damages of climate change?

Basic needed information

According to the Intergovernmental Panel "Taken as a whole, the range of published evidence indicates that the net damage costs of climate change are likely to be significant and to increase over time.

We have to stop climate change as soon as we can to ensure a safe and healthy planet for future generations.
