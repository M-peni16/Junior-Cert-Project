Preventing Climate Change

Although we are starting to acknowledge the impact of Climate Change. This however is not enough!

Sustainable development is where demands of the environment can be met without harming the future of the planet

Here are some ways to be sustainable

Being Sustainable

Use renewable energy sources instead of non-renewable energy sources

Recycle waste and materials

Reuse materials when possible

Reduce the amount of unnecessary plastic in your household

More ways to help

Use public transport instead of a personal car

Car pool with a friend

Walk or cycle to school
