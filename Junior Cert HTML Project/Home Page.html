The Basics

What is Climate Change?

Climate Change refers to a broad range of global issues involving weather patterns.

Changes in weather include rise in sea level, extreme weather events and loss shifts in flower or plant blooming!

How does Climate Change differ from Global Warming?

Global warming is the increasing temperature trend across the globe. This is because of the increase in greenhouse gas production.

Earths natural function is to keep in heat that is lost from Earth. This is known as the greenhouse effect

What speeds up global warming?

Global warming is sped up by:

The burning of fossil fuels

Deforestation
