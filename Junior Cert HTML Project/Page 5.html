The Impact on Animals

We have spoken a lot on the impact of humans but sometimes

we forget the animals who breath the same air, drink the same water and walk on the same soil as us

Animals

As we previously mentioned, a rise in sea level is an impact of climate change. This leads to the destruction of many animal habitats

Warming oceans can also ruin the lives of many sea creatures

In places like the Amazon rain forest which houses many species like the macaw, the jaguar and the black spider monkey, warmer temperature only leads to problems

Droughts happen more frequently and as a result so many animals have no home, food or even water!

How to help animals and their homes

Express your anger about this topic

Adopt an animal

Pay money to shelters that help those animals

Go meatless once a week
